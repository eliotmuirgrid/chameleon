# Does AI weaken thinking?

AI can weaken thinking when you use it to replace judgment instead of support judgment.

The risk is highest when someone copies output without checking facts, fit, or intent.
In that pattern, the person stops practicing clarity, selection, and accountability.

AI can also strengthen thinking when used as a structured draft partner.
You still decide what matters, reject weak suggestions, and revise for the real audience.

A simple rule: if your review step is optional, quality and thinking will decay; if review is required, AI can increase communication quality without giving up ownership.
