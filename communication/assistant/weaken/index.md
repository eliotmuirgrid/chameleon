# When assistants weaken communication

Assistants weaken communication when they replace your intent instead of helping you express it.

This usually happens when you send text you do not understand, skip review, or stop adapting the message to the audience.

Communication also weakens when speed is valued over accuracy, ownership, and context.

Assistants strengthen communication when you stay the author: set the intent, review the output, and revise for the real audience and situation.
