# No plural names in `concepts/` paths

## Concept

Do not use plural words for path segments under `concepts/`.
Plural names often hide the page job: one hub, many peers, or mixed content.
Singular names are clearer.

## What to do instead

Use singular segment names and link out to child pages.
If you need a collection, make it explicit with structure.
