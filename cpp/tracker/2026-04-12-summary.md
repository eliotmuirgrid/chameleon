## Family breakfast (together) — anchor meal

You **eat breakfast as a family**, so **breakfast is planned first** and should carry a **large share** of the day: roughly **~35–45% of calories** and a **big chunk of protein** for each person. **Lunch and dinner** are **lighter or simpler** unless you still have macros to close.

| | **Rough breakfast share of day** | **Then** |
|---|----------------------------------|----------|
| **Eliot** | **~650–800 kcal** toward **≤2000** · **~55–70 g protein** toward **180 g** | Lunch + dinner + any snack **split the remainder** |
| **Subrina** | **~750–900 kcal** toward **~2200** · **~40–50 g protein** toward **115 g** | Lunch + dinner + optional snack **split the remainder** |

**Post-workout:** When training lines up with the morning, **stack recovery food into breakfast** (oats, fruit, protein) so **one big family meal** covers both.
