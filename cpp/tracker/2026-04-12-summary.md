### Jerk marinade — *if spoon sizes were wrong*

Logged amounts use **one spoon ≈ volume of 4.5 g olive oil** (~**4.9 ml**): **~11 g** paste + **~6 g** ketchup + **~7 g** honey → **~38 kcal** and **~10 g carbs** for the **whole marinade**, split **61% / 39%** with the chicken (**~23 / ~15 kcal**, **~6 / ~4 g C** to Eliot / Subrina).

**How errors propagate (good enough for planning):**

- **Roughly linear in grams.** If every spoon were **10% bigger** than you thought, total marinade kcal and carbs scale about **+10%** (~**+4 kcal** and **+1 g C** to the batch). **20% off** → about **±8 kcal** and **±2 g C** on the batch.
- **Honey moves the needle most.** It is **~half** the marinade calories and **~60%** of the marinade carbs; uncertainty on the **honey** spoon changes the total more than the same **%** error on **tomato paste** or **ketchup**.
- **Ketchup vs label.** Real ketchup varies a bit by brand; **`foods.tsv`** uses a generic **~1.12 kcal/g** — a few percent either way is normal.
