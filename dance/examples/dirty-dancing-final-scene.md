# Dirty Dancing final scene

The big dance scene in *Dirty Dancing* is the final performance of "(I've Had) The Time of My Life."

Video: [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uWbybukpdCU)

Why it stands out:

- Strong build from simple steps to high-energy movement.
- Clear partner connection and timing.
- Big emotional payoff for the story and the audience.
- Iconic final lift that people remember.
