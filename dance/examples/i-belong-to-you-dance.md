# I Belong to You dance

This example uses "I Belong to You" as a lyrical dance reference with a close emotional feel.

Video: [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v2jDMwQ7Caw)

Why it stands out:

- Smooth tempo that supports flowing movement.
- Strong emotional tone for expressive dance.
- Works well for duet or solo interpretation.
- Easy to build from simple steps to full phrasing.
