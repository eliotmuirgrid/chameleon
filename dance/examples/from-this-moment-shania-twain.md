# From This Moment On dance

This example uses "From This Moment On" by Shania Twain as a slow, emotional dance moment.

Video: [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a-Lp2uC_1lg)

Why it stands out:

- Strong focus on connection between partners.
- Clear rhythm that supports steady movement.
- Emotional tone that fits romantic choreography.
- Easy to adapt for a staged performance.
