# A Thousand Years dance

This example uses "A Thousand Years" as a slow dance reference for emotional storytelling.

Video: [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rtOvBOTyX00)

Why it stands out:

- Very clear phrasing for choreographing sections.
- Gentle tempo that supports controlled movement.
- Strong romantic mood for expressive performance.
- Popular song choice that audiences recognize.
