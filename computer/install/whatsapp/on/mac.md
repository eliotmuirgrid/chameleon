# Install WhatsApp on an Apple computer

These steps are for **Apple laptops and desktops** running **macOS**. If you are new to those terms, read [Apple computers (read this if any of this is new)](index.md#apple-computers-read-this-if-any-of-this-is-new) first.

**Goal:** get the official **WhatsApp** app onto your computer and open it once, so you can chat from the desktop when you want.

You need **WhatsApp already working on your phone** (the mobile app). The computer app is an extra screen for the same account—it does not replace the phone setup.

---

## Easiest path: App Store

The **App Store** is Apple’s built-in shop for apps on macOS.

1. Open the **App Store** (for example: click its icon in the **Dock**—the strip of icons at the bottom or side of the screen—or open it from search; see [Open apps with search](../../../open/apps/with/spotlight/mac.md)).
2. Search for **WhatsApp**.
3. Choose **WhatsApp** from **WhatsApp Inc.** (check that the publisher looks right).
4. Click **Get** or **Install**. Sign in with your **Apple ID** (the account you use for Apple services) if asked, and wait for the download to finish.
5. Open **WhatsApp** from **Launchpad** (an app that shows all your apps) or from the **Applications** folder in **Finder**.

---

## Other path: WhatsApp website

1. In a web browser (for example **Safari**, which comes with macOS, or **Chrome**), go to [https://www.whatsapp.com/download](https://www.whatsapp.com/download).
2. Pick the download **for Mac** (Apple’s computers) and open the file you get.
3. Drag **WhatsApp** into **Applications** if the window asks you to, then open it from Applications.

Use the website if you prefer not to use the App Store; stay on WhatsApp’s own site so you get the real app.

---

## First time you open it

The app will ask you to **link** this computer to your phone—often by scanning a **QR code** with WhatsApp on your phone (**Settings → Linked devices** on the phone, then scan). Follow the on-screen steps. After that, your chats can show up on the computer.
