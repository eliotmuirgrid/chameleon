# Music (concepts)

**Summary:** In this notebook, **music** is **organised sound** in time: **rhythm**, **melody**, **harmony**, and **timbre** that people use for **feeling**, **memory**, **identity**, and **togetherness** in a room, in a community, or alone with headphones. These pages are **not** a theory curriculum; they **name ideas** you can **reuse** and **link** to other concept notes.
