# Why muscle energy matters

## Concept

Muscle energy use matters for three connected reasons:

1. Glucose and insulin handling
2. Total energy balance
3. Training adaptation over time

Resting use sets a floor.
Activity adds variable demand.
Together they shape how fuel from food is used.
