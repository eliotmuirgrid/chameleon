# Muscle and blood glucose uptake

## Concept

Active muscle can take glucose from blood with less reliance on insulin than resting tissue.
This helps movement lower or flatten post-meal glucose rises.

Muscle is a major glucose sink.
So both movement level and muscle mass affect day-to-day glucose handling.

## Why

- It links movement to blood sugar control.
- It explains why light activity after meals can help.
- It connects metabolism, training, and nutrition.
