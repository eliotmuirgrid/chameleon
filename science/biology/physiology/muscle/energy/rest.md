# Muscle energy at rest

## Concept

Muscle is metabolically active even when you are still.
It spends energy on cell upkeep, protein repair, heat, and muscle tone.

Muscle tissue uses more energy at rest than fat tissue.
So, more muscle mass usually raises resting energy use.
The size of that increase is real but modest.

## Why

- It explains why muscle is not "off" between workouts.
- It supports daily baseline energy use.
- It affects energy balance over time.
