# Muscle energy during movement

## Concept

When muscle contracts, energy demand rises quickly.
The body must make ATP faster.
It pulls more from glucose and fat.
During sustained activity, oxygen-supported pathways do more of the work.

Recovery also costs energy.
After hard work, the body uses energy for repair and replenishment.

## Why

- Activity energy use is variable and can be large.
- It combines with resting use to shape total daily burn.
- It links training load with fuel use.
