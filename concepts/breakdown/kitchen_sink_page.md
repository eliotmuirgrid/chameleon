# Kitchen sink page

## Concept

A kitchen sink page is one file that mixes many related ideas.
It feels convenient, but it makes reading and editing harder.

## Why it hurts

- Higher [cognitive load](../system/design/cognitive_load/README.md)
- Weak boundaries between ideas
- Fragile links to headings inside long files

This often overlaps with an [overloaded concept](overloaded_concept.md).

## Better pattern

Use a small hub page plus separate identity pages.
That follows [Concept - Detail Pages](detail.md) and [Recursive concept decomposition](breakup_concepts_into_web.md).

## Linking

Link to real files, not deep anchors in long pages.
Use [inline links](inline_links.md) and [relative links](relative_links.md).

Example: link to [ext4](../computer/storage/common_filesystems/ext4.md) as its own page.
