# HFS+

Optional identity for [Common filesystems (cross-platform)](../common_filesystems.md). For **case** behavior in context, see [Filesystem case on major desktop OS families](../../git/git_core_ignorecase/filesystem_case_by_os.md).

---

## Concept

**HFS+** (also called **Mac OS Extended**) is an **older Apple** filesystem still seen on **external** drives and older Macs.

## Case (typical)

Like typical [APFS](apfs.md) for consumers, it is commonly **case-insensitive** and **case-preserving**.
