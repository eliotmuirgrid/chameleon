# Psoas (Hip Flexor Stretch)

**Category:** Stretch

---

Step one foot forward into a lunge position with your back knee on the floor. Keep your torso upright and gently push your hips forward while maintaining a slight pelvic tilt (tuck hips). You should feel a stretch in the front of the back hip. Breathe steadily and avoid arching your lower back.

Purpose: This stretch opens the hip flexors, which often become tight from sitting. Improving this mobility helps posture, reduces lower back strain, and improves movement in walking and lunging.
