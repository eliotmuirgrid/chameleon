# Piriform (Figure-4 Stretch)

**Category:** Stretch

---

Lie on your back and cross one ankle over the opposite knee. Reach through and pull the supporting leg toward your chest until you feel a stretch deep in the glute of the crossed leg. Keep your shoulders relaxed and avoid pulling too hard.

Purpose: This stretch targets the piriformis and deep glute muscles, helping reduce tightness that can affect hip mobility and sometimes contribute to lower back or sciatic discomfort.
