# Posterior pelvic tilt crunch

**Category:** Stabilization

---

Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Start by taking a breath in, then slowly exhale as if you're fogging up a mirror. As you exhale, gently tilt your pelvis backward so your lower back flattens into the floor.

From that engaged position, continue the exhale and lift your head, shoulders, and upper torso slightly off the ground, keeping your lower back pressed down. The movement should feel like your ribcage is drawing toward your pelvis—not yanking your head forward.

Lift only as far as you can without losing the abdominal tension or flattening of the spine. Pause briefly at the top, then lower back down slowly, one segment at a time, maintaining control the entire way.
