# Muscle and calories at rest

Muscle burns calories even at rest because it is metabolically active tissue. Your body is always performing background work—maintaining cells, regulating ions, repairing tissue—and muscle requires more energy to sustain than fat. This contributes to your **basal metabolic rate (BMR)**, the calories you burn just to stay alive.

When we want to lose weight it's important not to lose muscle.
