# Adaptive communication

## Concept

Adaptive communication means changing your communication style to fit the other person, while keeping the core message true.

A flexible communicator can adjust:

- speed,
- detail level,
- tone,
- structure,
- and format (spoken, written, list, example).

## Why

People process information differently.

If you use only one style, some people will miss the message.

When style adapts to the listener, understanding is faster and mistakes drop.

## What does not change

Adaptive communication does not mean changing facts.

The message stays the same.
Only the delivery changes.

## Quick test

After explaining, ask:

1. Did they understand the core point?
2. Can they act on it without extra clarification?
3. If not, can I restate it in a different style?
