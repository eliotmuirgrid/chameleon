# Equilibrium - Subconcepts and examples

These pages show the same equilibrium pattern in different systems.

Layout follows [Concept - Detail Pages](../../../concept/detail.md): short hub on `equilibrium.md`, optional depth here.

## Examples

- [Osmosis](osmosis.md) - concentration differences drive flow until a balance point is reached.
- [Arbitrage](arbitrage.md) - price differences attract trades until the gap narrows.
- [Migration](migration.md) - population flow continues until push and pull forces balance.
- [CO2 bubbles coming off liquid](co2_bubbles.md) - dissolved gas leaves until rates in and out balance.
- [Eliot's mental equilibrium while assimilating concepts](eliot_mental_equilibrium.md) - a person needs enough space and processing time to stabilize understanding.
