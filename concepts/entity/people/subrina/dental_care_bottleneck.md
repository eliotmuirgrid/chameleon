# Example: relieving a bottleneck (dental care)

This page is a **worked example** of a [bottleneck](../../../pareto/general/bottleneck.md) in a real situation—how it showed up, how it was **relieved**, and how that ties to [**theory of constraints**](../../../pareto/general/theory_of_constraints.md). It sits in context of [Subrina's job description](../subrina.md).

---

## What happened

Subrina took initiative to ensure the CEO scheduled a **dental checkup**—something that had been overlooked for **more than two years**. She did not only flag the issue; she took **full ownership** of the administrative process: coordinating the appointment, gathering insurance details, and managing the logistics **end to end**.

She also navigated the **financial constraints** of a **minimal health plan**—one chosen mainly to meet **Cayman Islands** legal requirements and offering **very little practical coverage**—by working **directly with providers** and negotiating **more favorable pricing**. What could have been a costly, neglected task became a **well-managed outcome**.

---

## Where the bottleneck actually was

This is [**theory of constraints**](../../../pareto/general/theory_of_constraints.md) **in action**. The limit on the system was not only "nobody put it on the calendar"; it included **getting the right information across at the right time**. **Over lunch**, Subrina was able to **communicate the appointment details** clearly to the CEO—so the bottleneck was relieved through a **real handoff** that let the rest of the system (follow-through, payment, showing up) actually run. Address **that** [bottleneck](../../../pareto/general/bottleneck.md), and overall flow improves.

---

## Broader fit

The same episode points to a **broader opportunity**: supporting **administrative and compliance** work tied to **employees in the Cayman Islands**. Subrina has a background in **accounting** and is familiar with the **local legal and regulatory** landscape, which positions her to take on more of that responsibility over time.

Overall, it illustrates her stepping in with **accountability** and **resourcefulness**—spotting a gap, owning it, and delivering a **practical, cost-conscious** result. With that background and initiative, she is well positioned to take an **increasingly larger role** in **operational and compliance** challenges as the system evolves.
