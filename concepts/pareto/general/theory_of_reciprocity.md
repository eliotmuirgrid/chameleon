# Theory of reciprocity

**Summary:** People expect a **fair exchange of value**. When that balance holds, **trust** and **stability** follow; when it does not, **motivation** and **trust** erode.

---

The **theory of reciprocity** is the idea that people expect a **fair exchange of value**. When someone gives time, effort, or skill, they expect something **meaningful** in return. That sense of balance is what builds **trust** and keeps **relationships** stable.

---

## Employment

In employment, the exchange is simple:

- The **employee** gives work, skill, and effort.
- The **employer** gives pay, opportunity, and respect.

When this exchange feels **fair**, people engage and do their best work. When it does not, motivation drops and trust breaks down.

A healthy workplace **maintains this balance over time**. It is not only a transaction—it is an **ongoing, fair exchange of value** between both sides.
