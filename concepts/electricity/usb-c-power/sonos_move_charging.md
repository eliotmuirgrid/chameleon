# Sonos Move charging (USB-C power)

## Concept

This is a real-world case of [USB-C power compatibility](../usb-c-power.md), showing that just having the right plug shape does not mean your device will charge—a good reminder that [symmetry in appearance does not guarantee function](../../system/design/symmetry.md).

I was under time pressure—trying to get the Sonos Move speakers to play music and set a mood for a romantic night with my partner.

I’d recently [decluttered](../../pareto/general/decluttering_and_retrieval.md): got rid of the proprietary Sonos charging stands, aiming for a simpler setup with only standard USB-C chargers—following the idea that one connector style, used everywhere, would make life easier.

But when I tried three different iPhone USB-C chargers, none of them worked for the Sonos Move. They were all [incompatible](../usb-c-power.md).

[This experience ended up teaching me much more about USB-C charging than I expected.](../usb-c-power.md)
