# Hamstring (Face Up, One Leg Extended)

**Category:** Stretch

---

Lie on your back with one leg bent and the other lifted straight up. Hold behind your thigh or calf and gently pull the straight leg toward you until you feel a stretch in the back of your leg. Keep the leg as straight as possible without locking the knee, and keep your lower back relaxed on the floor. Breathe slowly and hold the stretch without forcing it.

Purpose: This stretch improves flexibility in the hamstrings, which helps with hip movement and reduces strain on the lower back. Tight hamstrings can limit movement and lead to compensation in other areas.
