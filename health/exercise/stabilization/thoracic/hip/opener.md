# Thoracic hip opener

**Category:** Stabilization

**Cues / notes:** Higher and slower - only 5 reps

---

Start on all fours with your hands under your shoulders and knees under your hips. Keep your spine neutral and core lightly engaged.

Place one hand behind your head, with your elbow pointing down toward the floor. Your other hand stays planted firmly on the ground.

From this position, begin by gently rotating your upper body so your elbow moves down and slightly inward (you may lightly "close" toward the supporting arm). Then reverse the motion and rotate your chest open, bringing your elbow up toward the ceiling.

The movement should come from your thoracic spine (mid-back) while your hips stay as still as possible. Move slowly and with control, opening only as far as you can without shifting your pelvis.

Repeat for reps, then switch sides.
