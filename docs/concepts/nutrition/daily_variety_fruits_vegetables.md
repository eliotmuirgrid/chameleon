# Daily variety of fruits and vegetables

**Summary:** Eating a **wide range** of **fresh** fruits and vegetables helps your body **run better with less effort**. **Variety** spreads nutrients, supports the **gut**, steadies **energy**, and—at a systems level—builds **resilience** for **long-term health**.

---

Eating a variety of fresh fruits and vegetables each day helps your body run better with less effort.

Different plants provide **different nutrients**, so variety naturally covers more of what your body needs. They also **feed different gut bacteria**, which supports **digestion**, **immunity**, and overall **stability** in the microbiome.

Because they are **high in fiber and water**, they help **regulate blood sugar** and **keep you full**, reducing spikes and crashes. Over time, their **antioxidants** also contribute to lowering risk of **chronic disease**.

---

## At a systems level

**Variety creates resilience, which leads to more stable, long-term health.**
