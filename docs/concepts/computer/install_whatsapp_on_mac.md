# Install WhatsApp on a Mac

**Goal:** get the official **WhatsApp** app onto your Mac and open it once, so you can chat from the desktop when you want.

You need **WhatsApp already working on your phone** (the mobile app). The Mac app is an extra screen for the same account—it does not replace the phone setup.

---

## Easiest path: Mac App Store

1. Open the **App Store** (click the blue icon in the Dock, or search “App Store” in Spotlight).
2. Search for **WhatsApp**.
3. Choose **WhatsApp** from **WhatsApp Inc.** (or the official listing that matches the publisher you expect).
4. Click **Get** or **Install**, sign in with your **Apple ID** if asked, and wait for the download to finish.
5. Open **WhatsApp** from Launchpad or Applications.

---

## Other path: WhatsApp website

1. In **Safari** or **Chrome**, go to [https://www.whatsapp.com/download](https://www.whatsapp.com/download).
2. Pick the **Mac** download and open the file you get.
3. Drag **WhatsApp** into **Applications** if the window asks you to, then open it from Applications.

Use the website if you prefer not to use the App Store; both routes should give you the real app when you stay on WhatsApp’s own site.

---

## First time you open it

The app will ask you to **link** this Mac to your phone—usually by scanning a **QR code** with WhatsApp on your phone (**Settings → Linked devices** on the phone, then scan). Follow the short on-screen steps. When that is done, your chats can show up on the Mac.
