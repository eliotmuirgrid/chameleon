# Herbs you eat as a first win (gardening)

**Summary:** Among the **most immediate** bottlenecks home gardening can address is this: you **want a fresh herb** you already like in food, and you **often do not have it** when you are cooking. **Cilantro** is a plain example. Growing that **one** herb for your own use closes a **small, repeated** gap between what the plate needs and what is in the kitchen.

---

The pattern matches a [bottleneck](../system/pareto/general/bottleneck.md) in a simple system: the slow or missing step is “**herb in hand, right now**,” not how fancy the garden looks.

**Transfer test:** the same idea applies to **basil**, **mint**, or any other herb you reach for **often**; cilantro names a concrete case.
